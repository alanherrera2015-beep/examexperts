Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Exam Experts ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website examexperts.org or contact us through our contact form.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you fill out our contact form or use our services:

  • Name — so we can address you properly.
  • Email address — to respond to your inquiry.
  • Phone number (optional) — if you choose to provide it so we can reach you by phone.
  • Message content — the details of your inquiry or tutoring request.

We also collect standard usage data automatically (IP address, browser type, pages visited) via Google Analytics to help us improve our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the tutoring services you request.
  • Send you relevant information about our services (only with your consent).
  • Improve our website and user experience through analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Contact form submissions are transmitted securely via encrypted connections (HTTPS) and delivered to our team through SendGrid, a trusted email delivery service. We retain contact inquiries only as long as necessary to respond to you and fulfill our service obligations.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data; however, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Phone Number & SMS Privacy Policy

  • Use of Information: We use your phone number to send SMS messages regarding your inquiry and customer care related topics.
  • Data Sharing: Mobile phone information or opt-in data collected will not be shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.
  • Opt-Out: Reply "STOP" to unsubscribe or "HELP" for assistance.
  • Message Disclosures: Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
  • Data Security: Your data is protected and retained only as needed.

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any services.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect non-personally identifiable information such as pages visited and session duration. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may link to or embed third-party services including:

  • Calendly — for session booking. View Calendly's Privacy Policy.
  • SendGrid — for email delivery. View Twilio SendGrid's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — for website analytics. View Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: examexpertscontact@gmail.com
  • Phone: 281-541-5928

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out:

  • Email: examexpertscontact@gmail.com
  • Phone: 281-541-5928
  • Hours: Monday–Sunday, 8 AM–10 PM EST
