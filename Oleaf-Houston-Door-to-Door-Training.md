# Exam Experts Door-to-Door Training (Houston Team)

## Purpose
This document prepares Exam Experts team members to run professional, safe, and effective door-to-door outreach in Houston.

## Team Goals
- Introduce Exam Experts services to local households.
- Generate qualified leads for tutoring and exam prep.
- Build trust in the Houston community through respectful outreach.
- Book follow-up consultations.

## Daily Field Checklist
1. Wear approved Exam Experts shirt/badge.
2. Carry flyers, lead forms, pens, and charged phone.
3. Confirm assigned neighborhood and route map.
4. Review target audience for the day.
5. Attend team huddle for script refresh and goals.

## Houston Outreach Standards
- Work in pairs whenever possible.
- Follow posted neighborhood rules and “No Soliciting” signs.
- Keep outreach hours to approved daytime/evening windows.
- Be polite, concise, and leave immediately when asked.
- Never block driveways, sidewalks, or doorways.

## Recommended Door Script
1. **Greeting:**  
   “Hi, I’m [Name] with Exam Experts. We help students improve exam scores with one-on-one tutoring.”
2. **Reason for visit:**  
   “We’re in the Houston area this week sharing free study resources and scheduling short consults.”
3. **Value statement:**  
   “Our students get structured plans, accountability, and exam-specific coaching.”
4. **Call to action:**  
   “Would you like a quick information sheet or to schedule a free consultation call?”

## Objection Handling (Simple Responses)
- **“Not interested.”**  
  “No problem—thanks for your time. I can leave a resource in case you need support later.”
- **“We already have tutoring.”**  
  “That’s great. We also offer exam-specific strategy sessions if extra support is ever needed.”
- **“Too expensive.”**  
  “Understood. We have multiple options and can match services to your budget during a free consultation.”
- **“Send me info.”**  
  “Absolutely—here’s our flyer and contact details. Would text or email be better for follow-up?”

## Lead Capture Requirements
For every qualified conversation, collect:
- Full name
- Best phone number
- Email (if provided)
- Student grade level / exam type
- Best contact time
- Notes on needs and urgency

## Safety and Professional Conduct
- Do not enter homes.
- End any interaction that feels unsafe.
- Escalate incidents to team lead immediately.
- Stay hydrated and weather-aware (Houston heat/rain conditions).
- Keep communication active in team group chat.

## End-of-Day Process
1. Submit all leads to team lead before closeout.
2. Mark hot/warm/cold lead status.
3. Report route coverage and key feedback trends.
4. Identify top objections and successful responses.
5. Confirm next-day assignments.

## Performance Targets (Daily)
- Doors approached
- Conversations started
- Qualified leads collected
- Consultations booked
- Follow-up completion rate

## Team Reminder
Represent Exam Experts with professionalism, consistency, and respect. Every conversation should leave the community with a positive impression of the brand.
